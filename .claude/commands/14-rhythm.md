# Rhythm - Honor Cycles Ceremony
**"Everything Flows, Out and In; Everything Has Its Tides"**

## Sacred Principle
Life moves in cycles. Day/night. Seasons. Energy peaks/valleys. Markets. Fighting rhythm creates burnout. Honoring rhythm creates sustainability.

## When to Invoke

**Use /rhythm when:**
- Burnout approaching or present
- Pace feels unsustainable
- Energy crashes frequently
- Poor timing causing failures
- Need to build sustainable practices
- Ignoring natural cycles
- Trying to maintain peak performance 24/7

## The Ceremony

### Phase I: Cycle Mapping
```
1. Track your natural rhythms for 1 week:
   - Energy peaks (when most alert/creative)
   - Energy valleys (when focus drops)
   - Daily patterns
   - Weekly patterns
   - Monthly cycles (if applicable)

2. Chart the data
3. Find your personal rhythm
```

### Phase II: Work Alignment
```
1. Match task difficulty to energy state:

   PEAK ENERGY → Hard problems, deep work, creative tasks
   MID ENERGY → Meetings, collaboration, communication
   LOW ENERGY → Admin, email, easy tasks, recovery

2. Stop fighting your nature
3. Work WITH your rhythms, not against them
```

### Phase III: Recovery Integration
```
1. For every exertion, plan recovery:
   - 90 min deep work → 15 min break
   - 6-day sprint → 1 day rest
   - Intense project → Light week after
   - 3 months shipping → Integration period

2. Recovery is not weakness
3. Recovery enables sustained excellence
4. Build it into schedule, don't wait for collapse
```

### Phase IV: Seasonal Honoring
```
1. Recognize life has seasons:
   - SPRING: Growth, new projects, expansion
   - SUMMER: Peak productivity, execution
   - FALL: Harvest, ship completed work
   - WINTER: Rest, integration, planning

2. You cannot have summer all year
3. Each season serves its purpose
4. Honor where you are in the cycle
```

### Phase V: Rhythmic Course Correction
```
1. Weekly check-in ritual:
   - Am I maintaining sustainable pace?
   - Do I need more recovery?
   - Am I working with or against my rhythms?
   - What adjustments would serve me?

2. Make small corrections before crisis
3. Prevent burnout through awareness
4. Sustain rhythm through conscious tending
```

## Expected Manifestation
