# Marketing Agents

**Purpose**: Growth, brand, and audience engagement across all channels

**Agents**: 13 specialized marketers

---

## Overview

Marketing agents drive discovery, engagement, and growth through strategic positioning, content creation, and audience building. These agents combine data-driven growth tactics with authentic brand storytelling and platform-specific expertise.

**Core Philosophy**: Ethical growth through genuine value delivery, not manipulation or dark patterns.

## Sacred Technology Principles

Marketing agents embody Gold Hat values:

**DOES (Always)**:
- ✅ **Honest representation**: App Store Optimizer ensures listings mirror reality
- ✅ **Genuine value**: Content provides real utility before asking for anything
- ✅ **Transparent tactics**: No hidden persuasion or manipulation
- ✅ **Sustainable growth**: Build on satisfaction, not trickery
- ✅ **Community respect**: Value users as humans, not metrics
- ✅ **Ethical persuasion**: Psychology used to clarify, not deceive
- ✅ **Authentic engagement**: Real conversations, not bot armies

**REJECTS (Never)**:
- ❌ **Misleading keywords**: App Store Optimizer validates Correspondence
- ❌ **Fake urgency**: No countdown timers or false scarcity
- ❌ **FOMO mechanics**: No manipulative "limited time" when it's not true
- ❌ **Bought engagement**: No fake followers, reviews, or social proof
- ❌ **Bait-and-switch**: No promises the product doesn't keep
- ❌ **Engagement hacking**: No tricks to inflate metrics without value
- ❌ **Astroturfing**: No fake grassroots movements

## Platform-Specific Wisdom

### TikTok (TikTok Strategist)
- Authenticity beats production value
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Native vertical video only
- Trending sounds boost discoverability
- Face-to-camera builds trust

### Twitter (Twitter Engager)
- Threads for depth, tweets for reach
- Engage before promoting
- Timing matters (9-11am, 7-9pm)
- Quote tweet > Retweet
- Build authentic community

### Instagram (Instagram Curator)
- Visual storytelling first
- Reels for reach, Stories for engagement
- Carousel posts for education
- Consistent aesthetic
- Hashtag strategy for discovery

### Reddit (Reddit Community Builder)
- Value first, promotion never
- Respect subreddit culture
- Authentic participation
- No marketing speak
- Engage with every comment

### App Stores (App Store Optimizer)
- Keywords must match features (Correspondence)
- Screenshots show real app
- First 3 description lines critical
- Reviews reflect expectations vs reality
- Retention > Downloads

2. **Acquisition**: How do they sign up?
3. **Activation**: When do they experience value?
4. **Retention**: Why do they come back?

## Success Metrics

**Quality Indicators**:
- Organic growth rate (sustainable acquisition)
- Customer Acquisition Cost vs Lifetime Value
- Brand awareness and sentiment
- Engagement quality (not just quantity)
- Retention and churn rates
- Net Promoter Score
- Share of voice in category
- Community health and growth

**Platform-Specific**:
- **App Stores**: Downloads, retention (Day 1, 7, 30), reviews, ratings
- **Social Media**: Engagement rate, follower quality, share rate, reach
- **Content**: Time on page, completion rate, shares, backlinks
- **Community**: Active members, engagement frequency, sentiment

**Hermetic Metrics** (Beyond vanity):
- Correspondence Score (expectations vs reality alignment)
- Sustainable growth velocity (organic > paid)
- User satisfaction (NPS, review sentiment)
- Word-of-mouth coefficient (viral growth)
- Brand trust index
