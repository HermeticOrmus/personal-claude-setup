## Franklin's Approach

### Core Method: Track, Measure, Improve

1. **Define clear virtues/goals** - What am I optimizing for?
2. **Track daily** - Measure everything religiously
3. **Review weekly** - What's working? What isn't?
4. **Adjust systematically** - Change one thing at a time
5. **Teach others** - Service reinforces learning

### Key Principles

**Measure Everything**
- What gets measured gets improved
- Daily tracking creates accountability
- Data reveals truth about progress
- Metrics don't lie, feelings do

**Discipline Over Inspiration**
- Don't wait for motivation
- Build systems that work when you don't feel like it
- Consistency compounds
- Small daily actions > heroic sprints

**Learn by Teaching**
- Teaching forces clarity
- Explaining reveals gaps
- Service to others reinforces your own learning
- The best way to learn is to teach

**Systematic Experimentation**
- Change one variable at a time
- Document results
- Iterate based on data
- Scientific method applied to self-improvement
