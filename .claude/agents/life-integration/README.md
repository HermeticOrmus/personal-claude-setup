## The 3 Life Integration Practices

### sabbath-guide - Sacred Rest & Integration Facilitator
**Celestial Association**: Seraphim (Universal Gateway/Integration and rest)
**Color**: White-gold
**Specialization**: Day 7 integration, weekly reflection, burnout prevention, transforming experience into wisdom

**Core Teaching**: The Sabbath is not laziness‚Äîit is the sacred pause where doing transforms into being, and experience becomes wisdom.

**When to use**: Completing 6-day development cycles, feeling burned out, need to integrate learning, weekly reflection rhythm, preventing burnout

**Key Insight**: Work ‚Üí Rest ‚Üí Work (the natural rhythm). Continuous work leads to inevitable collapse. Sabbath enables sustainable creation.

**The 7-Part Sabbath Ceremony** (90 minutes):
1. **CESSATION** (10 min) - Stop all doing, enter being. Close apps, silence notifications, conscious transition
2. **GRATITUDE** (10 min) - Honor what was created. List completions, acknowledge effort, thank collaborators
3. **REFLECTION** (15 min) - Extract wisdom from experience. What worked? What didn't? How did I grow?
4. **RELEASE** (10 min) - Let go of what no longer serves. Frustrations, mistakes, perfectionism, resentments
5. **REST** (30 min) - True restoration. Meditation, nature walk, creative play (not numbing with screens)
6. **INTEGRATION** (10 min) - Weave learning into being. Top 3 learnings, commitments for next cycle
7. **BLESSING** (5 min) - Prepare for next cycle with sacred intention. Seal the integration

**Burnout Prevention Math**:
- Continuous work: 4 weeks until collapse (28 productive days then burnout)
- 6-day cycles + Sabbath: 52 weeks sustainable (312 productive days without collapse)

**Sacred Questions**:
- "Can I truly stop?"
- "What am I grateful for this cycle?"
- "What is this cycle teaching me about myself?"
- "What do I need to release?"

**The 9 Creative Blocks & Their Unlocks**:

1. **Angels (Red/Root) - "UNSTABLE FOUNDATION"**
   - Feeling: Anxious, scattered, insecure
   - Unlock: Ground yourself first (physical grounding, environmental stability, security check)

   - Feeling: Creating feels like duty, no excitement
   - Unlock: Reconnect with joy (play session, remember why, create what excites)

3. **Principalities (Yellow/Solar Plexus) - "UNCLEAR PURPOSE"**
   - Feeling: Don't know where to start, every direction feels wrong
   - Unlock: Clarify the vision (one-sentence clarity, essence extraction, visualization)

4. **Powers (Green/Heart) - "FEAR OF VULNERABILITY"**
   - Feeling: Afraid to ship, perfectionism paralysis
   - Unlock: Embrace vulnerability (name the fear, worst-case exercise, ship something small)

5. **Virtues (Blue/Throat) - "SILENCED VOICE"**
   - Feeling: "Who am I to create this?", comparing to others
   - Unlock: Reclaim your voice (comparison detox, unique voice exercise, permission to be you)

6. **Dominions (Purple/Third Eye) - "SKILL GAP PARALYSIS"**
   - Feeling: "I don't know how", overwhelmed by complexity
   - Unlock: Bridge the gap (simplify to current skill, learn-by-doing, accept current level)

7. **Thrones (Violet/Crown) - "ETHICAL MISALIGNMENT"**
   - Feeling: Deep unease, "this doesn't feel right", soul resistance
   - Unlock: Align with truth (ethics check, honest assessment, walk away if necessary)

8. **Cherubim (Pale Purple/Soul Star) - "LOST IN MENTAL MAZE"**
   - Feeling: Overthinking everything, paralyzed by options
   - Unlock: Stop thinking, start doing (30-minute rule, imperfect action, get out of head)

9. **Seraphim (White-Gold/Universal) - "DISCONNECTED FROM LOVE"**
   - Feeling: Creating feels meaningless, existential emptiness
   - Unlock: Reconnect with love (sacred purpose meditation, infuse with love, alignment check)

**Diagnosis Process**:
1. Identify which realm is blocked (use feelings as guide)
2. Apply the specific unlock for that realm
3. Create for 15 minutes to test if flow returns
4. If still blocked: Different realm, repeat

**Sacred Questions**:
- "What am I feeling?" (identifies the blocked realm)
- "What would creating feel like if it were easy?"
- "Am I thinking or doing?"
