## What Was Added

Five new resources that ground the Hermetic philosophy in economic and practical reality:

### 1. **QUICK-START.md** - The Essential 20%
**Purpose:** Stop overwhelming newcomers with 103 agents

**What it provides:**
- Core question (Empower or Extract?)
- 3 essential principles (Vibration, Cause-Effect, Rhythm)
- 5 essential commands (/gnosis, /align, /verify, /dev-docs, /celebrate)
- 3 essential agents (strategic-plan, filesystem-org, sacred-mirror)
- Essential anti-patterns to reject
- Daily workflow

**When to use:** Starting point for everyone. Read this before diving deep.

---

### 2. **CONFLICT-RESOLUTION.md** - When Principles Collide
**Purpose:** Navigate the tension between competing goods

**What it provides:**
- 5 common conflicts (Vibration vs Cause-Effect, Rhythm vs Pressure, etc.)
- Decision frameworks for each conflict
- How to sequence instead of sacrifice
- When to break your own rules (consciously)
- Template for making hard decisions

**When to use:** When you feel torn between two valid Hermetic principles, or between ideals and constraints.

**Key insight:** Mastery isn't eliminating tension - it's dancing with it consciously.

---

### 3. **ECONOMIC-REALITY.md** - Ethics Under Financial Pressure
**Purpose:** Maintain integrity while paying rent

**What it provides:**
- 4-tier hierarchy (Survival → Stability → Selectivity → Mission Alignment)
- How to define your hard boundaries
- Business model spectrum (high to low alignment)
- Practical strategies for building aligned income
- What to do when clients demand dark patterns
- When to compromise vs when to walk away
- The gradual transition path (Year 1-4 roadmap)

**When to use:** When facing economic pressure that conflicts with Gold Hat values.

**Key insight:** You can't practice ethics if you're homeless. Survival first, then build the runway that enables integrity.

---

### 4. **RETROSPECTIVE-TEMPLATE.md** - Measurement & Accountability
**Purpose:** Verify you're actually living the principles, not just believing you are

**What it provides:**
- Weekly quick reflection (15 min)
- Monthly Hermetic audit (30 min)
- Quarterly deep alignment review (60 min)
- Principle scorecard (rate 1-10 on each)
- Economic alignment audit (weighted by income %)
- Dark pattern check
- Drift warnings (red alerts)
- Metrics dashboard (track over time)

**When to use:**
- Weekly: Friday afternoons
- Monthly: Last Friday of month
- Quarterly: End of Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4

**Key insight:** Unexamined work leads to unconscious drift. What gets measured gets mastered.

---

### 5. **SYSTEM-MAP.md** - Visual Navigation
**Purpose:** Show how all pieces connect and where to start

**What it provides:**
- ASCII visual diagram of complete system
- Entry points by use case (new project, ethical dilemma, burnout, etc.)
- Decision flow chart
- Principle → Tool → Outcome mapping
- Progressive mastery path (Month 1 → Year 2+)
- Quick reference guide

**When to use:** When overwhelmed or lost. Shows you the forest when you're stuck in the trees.
