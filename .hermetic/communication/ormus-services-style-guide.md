## Response Time Expectations

### **By Client Type:**

**Established Clients:**
- Same-day for questions
- Immediate for critical issues
- Weekly updates on ongoing projects (unless milestone-based)

**New Clients:**
- Within 4 hours during work hours
- Within 24 hours on weekends
- Set expectations upfront if you batch responses

**VIP/High-Stakes:**
- Within 1 hour during work hours
- Same day on weekends
- Proactive updates (don't wait to be asked)

### **Setting Boundaries:**

**Upfront Communication:**
```
I check messages twice daily (10am/4pm Panama time).

For urgent issues: Call me at [number].

Weekly updates: Every Friday by 5pm.
```

**Don't apologize for:**
- Having boundaries
- Not responding instantly
- Working sustainable hours

**Do communicate:**
- Your response rhythm
- How to reach you for emergencies
- When they can expect updates
