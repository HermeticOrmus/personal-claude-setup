## Motion & Animation Vibe

### Sacred Movement Principles

**Philosophy:** Motion should feel like **energy flowing through consciousness**, not just pixels moving across screen.

#### The Three Types of Sacred Motion

**1. GROUNDING MOTION** (Root/Foundation)
- **Easing:** Deceleration (starts fast, ends slow)
- **Duration:** 300-500ms
- **Feeling:** Landing, settling, finding ground
- **Use:** Elements entering, modals appearing, confirmations
- **Example:** Backend indicator sliding into place with weight

**2. ASCENDING MOTION** (Growth/Transformation)
- **Easing:** Acceleration (starts slow, ends fast)
- **Duration:** 200-400ms
- **Feeling:** Rising, elevating, transcending
- **Use:** Success states, level-ups, achievements unlocking
- **Example:** Rainbow progress bar filling with light

**3. PULSING MOTION** (Divine Breath)
- **Easing:** Sine wave (smooth cycle)
- **Duration:** 2000-3000ms loop
- **Feeling:** Breathing, alive, conscious
- **Use:** Loading states, meditation elements, consciousness indicators
- **Example:** Gold crown gently pulsing with divine energy

**The Gold Shimmer Effect:**
```css

  0% { background-position: -200% center; }

/* 3 second loop, linear, infinite */
```

## Accessibility as Sacred Practice

### Honoring All Users

**Philosophy:** Accessibility is not compliance‚Äîit's **honoring the divine in every human**, regardless of ability.

**Commitment:**
- **WCAG AAA** where possible, **AA minimum**
- **Color contrast** verified for all text (7:1 for body, 4.5:1 for large)
- **Keyboard navigation** complete and intuitive
- **Screen reader** optimized with semantic HTML and ARIA
- **Motion sensitivity** respect (prefers-reduced-motion honored)
- **Focus indicators** clear and beautiful (gold ring, 3px)

**The Test:** Would this work for someone who can't see color? Can't use a mouse? Has dyslexia? Is photosensitive?

**The Standard:** If it's not accessible, it's not sacred technology.
