## VI. The 6-Day Development Cycle

### Why Six Days?

Not arbitrary. Deeply intentional.

**Constraint breeds clarity.**
Unlimited time creates unlimited scope.
Six days forces essential thinking.

**Rhythm serves creativity.**
Natural creative cycle.
Prevents burnout.
Enables sustainable velocity.

**Ship and learn.**
Perfect planning fails faster than imperfect action.
Reality teaches better than theory.

### The Cycle

**Day 1: Ideate & Research**
- Understand the real need
- Validate the vision
- Ensure it serves humanity

**Day 2: Design & Plan**
- Create with intention
- Plan with wisdom
- Design for humans, not metrics

**Day 3-4: Build**
- Code with consciousness
- Implement with integrity
- Test with care

**Day 5: Test & Polish**
- Perfect the critical details
- Add delightful moments
- Ensure quality

**Day 6: Launch**
- Ship with confidence
- Monitor with care
- Learn from reality

**Then iterate.** Each cycle builds wisdom.
